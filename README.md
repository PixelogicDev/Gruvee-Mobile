## ü§î Why use it?

Gr√ºvee is being created to make sharing music an easier and more personal experience with others. While at the same time, getting around the plethora of music streaming platforms out there. Gr√ºvee does this by addressing the following concerns:

-   üéµ `Different Music Services`: Gr√ºvee will give you the opportunity to collaborate on playlists with people that don't share the same music streaming service as you! Behind the scenes, Gr√ºvee will make sure you are all in sync with your favorite platform.
-   üí¨ `Discussions`: Talk about what gets your HYPED about certain songs and save that thread with your friends! Gr√ºvee will keep track of your comments on a per song basis.
-   üîî `Notifications`: Get notified when people in your Gr√ºvee Playlist do something! It's funny how major providers don't even notify you when people add new songs to your collaborative playlists. Gr√ºvee is here to change that.
-   üîä `Show Off Your Music Taste`: Music is an expression of ourselves. Gr√ºvee is focused around how to you express your love and passion for music in a way that is fun and inspiring.
